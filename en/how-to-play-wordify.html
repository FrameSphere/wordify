Keyboard controls

On-screen keyboard

Wordify displays a full keyboard below the grid. The on-screen keyboard also colors already-used letters so you can see at a glance which letters haven't been tested yet, which are in the word, and which have been eliminated.

You type by simply tapping letters. The backspace arrow deletes the last letter. The Enter button submits your word.

Hardware keyboard

On desktop or laptop you can also use your hardware keyboard. Just type the letters on your keyboard. The Backspace key deletes the last letter, and the Enter key submits your word.

What happens if a word is not in the dictionary?

Wordify only accepts real, valid English words. If you enter a word that isn't in the word list, a short error message like "Not in word list" appears and your input is reset. You don't lose an attempt â€“ you can simply enter a different word.

This means you can't enter random letter combinations to quickly test all possible letters. Every attempt must be a real word. That makes the game more challenging, but also fairer.

Daily puzzle vs. random game

Wordify offers two game modes:

The daily puzzle is the official word of the day, the same for all players worldwide. A new daily puzzle is unlocked every day at midnight. The daily puzzle contributes to your statistics and can only be played once per day.

The random mode lets you play additional rounds with randomly selected words. This mode is limited to 15 games per 12 hours so that Wordify remains a conscious daily ritual rather than an endless pastime.

How do the statistics work?

After every completed game, your results are saved locally in your browser. Via the statistics button in the game you can view your personal stats. You'll see:

Games played: The total number of completed rounds.

Win rate: The percentage of games you've won. A good rate is above 80%.

Current streak: How many consecutive days you've won at least once.

Max streak: Your personal record of consecutive winning days.
