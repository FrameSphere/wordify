What is Wordify? The Daily 5-Letter Word Puzzle

Wordify is a free online word game that challenges your vocabulary and logical thinking skills. Every day, a new 5-letter word awaits you, and you have a maximum of 6 tries to guess it. The unique feature: After each guess, you receive visual feedback through colors.

Green letters indicate that the letter is correct and in the right position. Yellow letters mean the letter appears in the word but is in the wrong position. Gray letters are not in the target word at all.

How to Play Wordify?

The game principle of Wordify is simple but challenging:

Step 1: Enter any English word with 5 letters. The word must be valid and exist in our dictionary.

Step 2: Press Enter and observe the color changes. Green means correct position, Yellow means correct letter but wrong position, Gray means the letter doesn't appear.

Step 3: Use the feedback to optimize your next guesses. You have a total of 6 tries to find the correct word.

Step 4: Did you guess the word? Congratulations! You can share your result or start a new game.

Tips and Strategies for Wordify

With the right strategy, you can significantly improve your success rate at Wordify:

Start with common letters: Use a word with common letters like E, A, R, S, and T in your first try. Examples are TEARS, STARE, or AROSE.

Avoid repetitions: Use each try to test new letters instead of repeating already excluded letters.

Pay attention to vowels: English words usually contain at least one vowel. Test vowels like A, E, I, O, U early.

Use yellow hints wisely: When a letter is shown in yellow, you know it appears in the word. Try different positions.
