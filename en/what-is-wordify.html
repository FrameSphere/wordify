How does the color system work?

The heart of Wordify is its color feedback system. After each valid word you submit, the letters are shown in one of three colors:

W
Green: The letter is correct and in the right position in the target word. Keep it there!
O
Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong position. Try it somewhere else.
X
Gray: The letter is not in the target word at all. You can eliminate it from future guesses.

This system lets you gradually close in on the target word. With each attempt you learn more about the word's composition and can make your next guess more precisely. That's what makes Wordify a true thinking game that rewards logical deduction.
